Dr Prabhu Jayagopal


Dr. Prabhu Jayagopal is a distinguished Professor in the department of Software & Systems Engineering in School of Computer Science Engineering and Information Systems in Vellore Institute of Technology, Vellore, Tamil Nadu, India whose innovative research has advanced the fields of Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence and IoT related with healthcare. Dr. Prabhu Jayagopal belong to beautiful city Kanchipuram in Tamilnadu, India, Dr. Prabhu Jayagopal pursued his undergraduate studies at the Vellore Institute of Technology, where he earned his B.tech degree in Information Technology in the year 2004. Inspired by his professors' research in computer science engineering, Dr. Prabhu Jayagopal went on to complete his Master's in 2007 and Ph.D. in Computer Science Engineering at the Sathyabama university, Tamilnadu, India, specializing in software systems in the year 2015. Dr. Prabhu's academic career has been characterized by a relentless pursuit of research in computer science engineering. Dr. Prabhu Jayagopal's research has made significant contributions to both academia and industry with interests in innovative methods of thinking and different type styles of teaching and learning of total 16 years of experience as 1 year from industry and 15 years from Teaching. His work on COVID-19 detection based on lung CT scan using deep learning techniques has been instrumental in improving performance and scalability. Additionally, his investigations into Emphasizing privacy and security of edge intelligence with machine learning for healthcare is significant one. Dr. Prabhu Jayagopal's research findings have been disseminated through numerous peer-reviewed publications in leading journals and conference proceedings, His papers are widely cited by researchers in the field and have influenced the design of next-generation computing systems over 104 papers in International Journals, Book Chapters and Conferences. He is also actively involved in interdisciplinary collaborations with colleagues in fields such as neuroscience and bioinformatics, exploring the intersection of computer science with other scientific disciplines. Dr. Prabhu Jayagopal's holds various professionals memberships like International Association of Computer Science and Information Technology (IACSIT), International Association of Engineers (IAENG), Computer Science teacher Association and so on. More likely Dr. Prabhu Jayagopal's Received Research grant on “Weather-based maize yield forecast in Saudi Arabia using statistical analysis and Machine learning” (Institutional Financing track-2020) is accepted and funded by Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Ministry of Education, King Faisal University, Saudi Arabia (Worth SAR30000). Looking ahead, Dr. Prabhu Jayagopal envisions a future where he filed a patent on “IoT Based Smart Infant Monitoring System” computing systems seamlessly integrate IoT and Artificial Intelligence to address society's most pressing challenges in healthcare. He remains committed to mentoring the next generation of computer scientists and engineers of successful 6 Doctoral scientists and 3 Pursuing Ph.D scholars), inspiring them to push the boundaries of what is possible in the world of computing. Dr. Prabhu Jayagopal successfully delivered handful of Guest Lecture talk and presented paper in various conferences held in US, Malaysia, Singapore and Srilanka. Dr. Prabhu Jayagopal's groundbreaking research in computer science engineering has positioned him as a leading figure in the field of machine learning and Artificial intelligence. Through his relentless pursuit of innovation and collaboration, he continues to shape the future of computing and inspire the next generation of researchers to make their mark on the world.
